Content

Home page

Welcome to MegaBites! Please check out all our menu items! All you have to do is select an item and let us know where to drop it off! Here at MegaBites, we believe in getting food to you when you want it, and for an affordable price! We strive to live up to our motto, "Your food, quick and cheap!" So sample our food items and enjoy your time here! Let us know what you think when you are done! Not sure where to start? Here are some of our customers' favorite items! -Our first highlight is our signature burger, the Mega Burger! Made with premium beef and a grilled bun, this sandwich is arguably our most famous item on our menu! You can choose between one patty or two, what condiments are on there, and what toppings you would like, including lettuce, onions, tomato, American cheese, and bacon! -Next, we have our chicken sandwich! This sandwich needs no introduction- with a delicious fried filet and grilled buns, this meal has become a fan favorite! Comes with pickles (but don’t worry, you can remove them!) as well as your choice of lettuce, onions, and tomato! See what all the fuss is about! -Up next is our personal pan pizza! With delicious cheese and sauce, we have a variety of options available, including meat lovers, veggie, or just regular pepperoni! With medium and large sizes available, this will surely help last you a few days, or you can share it with others! -New to our menu is our all-day breakfast items! Choose from a biscuit with sausage, hash browns, pancakes, egg muffins, and more! It's always time for breakfast at MegaBites! -We also have salads available, with Caesar salad available as well! On top of all of this, we have a rewards program you can apply for after ordering your food and leaving a review! So order now and enjoy!
